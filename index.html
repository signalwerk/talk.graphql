Why?

Advantage

  • Standard FE-Workflow (no Drupal Knowledge)
  • Transitions betwen Pages
  • Ideal for Pages with high interactions
  • Ideal for Pages with State

Disadvantage

  • Additional Server
  • Many advantages to get feature parity with current Drupal Workflow

When?

  • Pages that mostly render and don't write
  • Drupal Commerce is probably not the right thing now...
